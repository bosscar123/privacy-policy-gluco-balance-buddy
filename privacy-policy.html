Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Diabetes Management Application.

Information We Collect

  • Blood glucose measurements
  • Carbohydrate intake records
  • User profile information (name, insulin ratio settings)
  • Device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized diabetes management features
  • To calculate and display statistics about your health
  • To improve our application and user experience
  • To store your preferences and settings

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures
  • Your data is encrypted during transmission
  • We regularly review our security practices
  • Your health data is stored locally on your device

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • Your data remains private and under your control

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your health records
  • Update your personal information
